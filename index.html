Evil Dead (2013)

Five friends are going to stay all the weekend on a cabin in the woods in Tennessee. The purpose is to help on of these people to recover from her fatal addiction to heroin, she even has been declared dead in a hospital for a few minutes, that is why they are taking such drastic actions this time. The group find a strange book which contains witchcraft knowledge and with this discovery Mia (who is the addict) starts to have strange visions and, the others think that Mia is suffering drug withdrawal, but this leads Mia to be possessed.

Megaman Zero

In an iron fist ruled empire called Neo Arcadia sentient robots and humans are supposed to live peaceful, but the major problem is when a robot breaks a rule or becomes disobedient, at this state it is called defective and is destroyed by the authorities. Seeing this, a human scientist called Ciel forms a robot resistance and escape from this empire. Neo Arcadia starts to hunt them, and when everything seems lost the resistance finds the chamber of the legendary robot warrior called Zero who has been nowhere seen since 100 years ago. Zero is awakened and has to fight with the resistance to defeat Neo Arcadia and destroy his old friend, X.

Kara no Kyoukai

After been 3 years in a coma Ryougi Shiki awakes with a strange power, the mistic eyes od death perception, now she is able to see some kind of lines in people and objects, having contact with these leads to this object's death. This is when Touko Aozaki offers the knowledge to use this new found powers in exchange of Shiki's aid in Touko's workshop. Then Shiki has to help in Garan no Dou paranormal combat cases while the truth of the incident which put her into a coma is unfold.
